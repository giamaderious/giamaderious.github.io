Graffiti Camp For Girls Mural, 2026

This mural was created by youth artists from Graffiti Camp for Girls, a program dedicated to developing artistic talent and expanding opportunities in public art for gender-expansive youth, including cis girls, trans girls, nonbinary, and other girl-identified participants. Through collaborative workshops, campers learn spray-painting techniques, mural design, and the history of street art while working together to create artwork for their communities. Projects like this encourage young artists to recognize their creative voices and understand how public art can shape shared spaces. By fostering collaboration, experimentation, and confidence, the program challenges the traditionally male-dominated culture of street art and helps ensure that the visual landscape reflects the diversity of the communities it represents. Graffiti Camp for Girls hosts programs throughout the Bay Area, including in San Francisco, Oakland, and San Jose.
