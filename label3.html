La Flaca, 2025

Sam Flores is an American visual artist, illustrator, and muralist, primarily creating urban- and graffiti-inspired modern art. Sam Flores was born in the state of New Mexico in the United States. As a teen, he moved to San Francisco, California. When he was younger, he was recorded to be inspired by the work of graffiti, which is what currently drives his inspirations for his paintings and works. He grew up in the scene of skating, graffiti, comic books, and hip hop music, all of which are reflected in his work. As of 2013, he is part of the Upper Playground, a San Francisco Bay Area brand collective of artists, with a sub-brand called 12Grain.
