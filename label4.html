Drifting Forest, Figure 1 2015

GATS is an anonymous street artist based in Oakland, known for the distinctive mask he wears while working. Although rooted in the Bay Area, his murals and graffiti appear internationally in places such as Palestine, the Philippines, and Italy, as well as in cities like Los Angeles and Seattle. While he exhibits in galleries—including representation by Hashimoto Contemporary—most of GATS’ work is created in public space without permission or payment. He views street art as a shared resource, believing that when art exists in the streets, it belongs to everyone who encounters it.
